Where we fit

Built for the stage between a spare bedroom and a 3PL contract.

Most order fulfillment services are built for volume you don’t have yet. Traditional 3PLs want monthly minimums, onboarding fees, and long contracts. Doing it yourself means your dining table doubles as a packing station and your growth is capped by your own two hands.

Packin’ Days sits in between. Your inventory moves to a dedicated partner in our network, your orders sync automatically from Shopify, and every order goes out the same way it would from your own home — just not from your home.

  • You’re currently packing orders yourself, or paying someone hourly to help.
  • Your order volume doesn’t yet clear a traditional 3PL’s minimums.
  • You want to swap fulfillment work for a single predictable line item.

Questions

What owners ask before signing up.

What if my order volume changes month to month?

There’s no minimum and no set volume commitment. You’re invoiced for exactly the orders packed that month, whether that’s twenty or two thousand.

Who owns the inventory once it’s shipped to a partner?

You do, the entire time. Your partner stores and packs it on your behalf, the same way it would sit in your own garage or spare room.

What happens if my partner needs to pause or step away?

We’ll coordinate moving your inventory to another partner in the network so your fulfillment continues without a gap in service.

Do I need to change anything about how I use Shopify?

No. Orders sync automatically once you’re connected, and shipping charges continue to bill through Shopify exactly as they do today.

What do I need to send when I sign up?

Your current inventory and your existing shipping label printer. Your partner packs and prints using the same setup you’d use at home.
